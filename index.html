Title

Roughly one million cars alone enter São Paulo daily. Creating and maintaining effective transportation infrastructure is a major issue relevent to cities across the world. However, there is no single path to improving mobility, even throughout areas of the same city. ColorScoPe allows for flexibility with an in-depth look at specific areas' road infrastructure, providing tools for innovative and effective urban management to address the needs of people, and maintain a vision of sustainable development.
